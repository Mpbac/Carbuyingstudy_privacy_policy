Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you participate in the Car Buying Study.

By using this enrollment form, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. Please read it carefully.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal and demographic information that you voluntarily provide to us through the enrollment form, including but not limited to:

  • Name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Demographic information (age, gender, race, education, occupation, income)
  • Geolocation data (latitude, longitude, accuracy)
  • Device and browser information (IP address)
  • Responses to screening questions and other survey questions

This information is collected solely for the purposes of this research study.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for research purposes, including:

  • To determine your eligibility to participate in the study.
  • To contact you regarding your participation and compensation.
  • To analyze car buying behaviors and demographics for academic research.
  • To prevent duplicate submissions and ensure data integrity.

Your data will be anonymized and aggregated for analysis wherever possible to protect your identity.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your information. We have implemented appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

Confidentiality

All information collected will be kept confidential. Your individual responses will not be shared with any third parties outside of the research team unless required by law. All data will be used in an aggregated and anonymized format for any publication or presentation of research findings.

Mobile Phone Numbers
